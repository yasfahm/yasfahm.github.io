DO YOU KNOW ?

Your personal data is worth $$

People shares everyday personal data without even realizing. While using GPS location, fitness application, music application or any other application through your device, you are sharing your personal information with the application itself.
Many companies are willing to buy big amount of personal data for their benefit.
In this application, we will analysis a case how personal data can be used.

Let's examine a Recipe Marketplace

We all know someone that has a great recipe or cook amazing food. Have you already asked someone to give you a recipe of the food they prepared? Most of as have!

Do you have a great food product or idea you want to launch into the marketplace?

Nowadays, when we search for a recipe, we google it or look into a recipe book. But now, by simple clicks you can access to the most amazing chef's receipe and you can even sell a receipe that you have created and tastes good.

Most of the people aren't aware that selling their personal data can make them a lot of money. In order to help people to get an idea of selling or buying some amazing recipes, we decided to create this marketplace application.


Analyze different user types

Customers have several needs when searching for a recipe. Some look for pricing, some for quantities and others for qualities. It is important to analyze your customers needs in order to make your sell, a recursive sell. Remember, as Jihm Rohn once said, if you make a sale, you can make a living. If you make an investment of time and good service in a customer, you can make a fortune. This is why we need to undertand users needs, experiences, behaviors and goals.

Three personas has been analysed. The first is about a person that cooks since she was young and would like to sell her recipes. The second persona is about a old couple who really like to try new recipes and is willing to pay for one recipe per month. The third person is simply someone who would like to find an alternative of always playing video games and would like to do more monney. He found that trying some recipes and sell them will make him make some extra money.

User Reseach

Know more about the people that you will be dealing with

Rule #1

While designing a UX website, it is important to understand the users that you will be dealing with
We decided to create a survey that we published on Facebook and with our friends to reach the maximum number of people. The purpose of this survey was to know more about the people that are aware that selling personal information exists and evaluate those that might be intrested in selling or buying recipes.

The following questions has been asked:

Do you know that your personal data is worth money?

Do you think big companies uses your data for their own benefit?

How do you feel now that you know that companies uses your data?

Would you sell your data in exchange of monetary compensation?

What personal data would you be willing to sell?

How much do you think is the value of your personal data

What type of food do you prefer?

How much are you willing to pay to get someone‘s good recipe?

Have you thought of selling one of your recipes that everyone loves?

Design explanation

Choice of color

The color orange has been chosen to design this application. This color is proved to make people feel hungy.

User friendly

This application may be used by different types of users. A 12 year old as a 60 year old may use this application without any confusion. By simple clicks, the user can have a look at his profile, his own recipes and his wallet. He can also see what is for sale and what he sold to keep track of the marketplace.
